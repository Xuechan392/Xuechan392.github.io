What are Field Notes?

This is not a formal blog. It's a space for thinking out loud — observations from coursework, ideas that don't fit in a paper, and occasional attempts to explain phonology to non-linguists without losing the interesting parts.

Some posts will be technical. Some will be personal. All of them are things I actually find interesting.
